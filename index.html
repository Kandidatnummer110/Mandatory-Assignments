Our story

Told from the owners perspective:

When I was little I dreamed of opening my own restaurant, and in the spring of 2002 "Restaurant Orange" opened its doors.

This restaurant has been a dearly beloved project of mine. I wanted to share the recipes of the wonderful women in my life with other food lovers.

For my family the kicthen has always been the place where people gathered: family, friends, neighbours, collegues and acquaintances. A place filled with love.

Food made with passion and love is something that I have carried with me into this restaurant.
